🛡️ How to Protect Yourself from QR Phishing:

  1. Always preview URLs before visiting - most QR scanners show the link
  2. Check for tampering - Look for stickers placed over legitimate QR codes
  3. Verify the source - Don't scan codes from unknown or unexpected sources
  4. Look for HTTPS - Ensure the website has a valid SSL certificate
  5. Use security software - Install QR scanners with URL checking
  6. Enable 2FA - Multi-factor authentication protects even if credentials are stolen
  7. Report suspicious QR codes - Notify security teams about potential threats
